Acquire a clear headspace.
Clear is an app that uses photos to analyze your mood and provides relevant tips on improving your mental health.
Hi
Quick and Simple to Use
No need to fill out logs or answer a ton of questions each day. All Clear needs is a daily image, and over a few days, you will be able to see an analysis report based on the analyses of these images.
Recommends What's Most Effective for You
There's no one solution to a poor mental health. Clear learns from your results and if it detects that a certain recommendation isn't too effective for you, it will provide other alternatives until it finds one that helps you the most.
Packed with Motivational Features
Mental health can sometimes feel boring and unnecessary, so Clear provides features that make it easier to keep up with your mental health.
